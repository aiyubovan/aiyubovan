Owner's Manual

How to update your site yourself

Everything you change regularly lives in two easy places. You never need to touch the design files, and you can't break the layout from here.

1

Open or close registration

Open site-content.js. Right at the top there is a settings block:

settings: {
  registrationOpen: false,          ← true = open, false = closed
  nextBatchDate: '2026 අගෝස්තු 21',  ← the date visitors see
  registrationOpensOn: '2026-08-07 09:00',  ← when registration OPENS (Sri Lanka time)
  registrationClosesOn: '2026-08-20 23:59', ← when registration ENDS
},

registrationOpensOn is the day and time you will OPEN registration — while registration is closed, the bar counts down to that moment. registrationClosesOn is the day and time registration ENDS — while registration is open, the bar counts down to that instead, and after it passes the bar says "ලියාපදිංචිය අවසන් වී ඇත". The countdown bar appears only on the Home, What You Will Learn, and Application Guide pages. Format: 'YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM' in Sri Lanka time.

When you set it to false, every "දැන්ම ලියාපදිංචි වන්න" button — homepage, nav bar, and application page — turns into a grey "ලියාපදිංචිය දැන් වසා ඇත" state, and a red NEXT BATCH STARTS ON stamp with your date appears on the price card, hero, application page and nav. Set it back to true two weeks before the batch and everything returns to normal — one word, whole site.
2

Add or edit cards and text (site-content.js)

Open the file site-content.js. It holds every list on the homepage: What Is Included, the 10 week curriculum, the pricing checklist, testimonials, and the FAQ. Each section has instructions right above it.

Testimonials never make the page long: add as many as you like — visitors see the first 6, and a "Show all reviews" button reveals the rest. Keep your best 6 at the top of the list.

Example — add a new "What Is Included" card:

included: [
  'ඕනෑම වෙලාවක නැවත බලන්න පුළුවන් Live Recordings',
  ...
  'ඔයාගේ අලුත් වාක්‍යය මෙතනට',   ← add your line here
],

Example — add a new FAQ:

{ q: 'ප්‍රශ්නය මෙතන', a: 'උත්තරය මෙතන' },
Three golden rules: keep text inside 'single quotes' · end every item with a comma · if your text needs a quote mark, write \' instead of '.
3

Good to know

  • The "What Is Included" section never makes the page endless — once the cards grow past a certain height, that section gets its own smooth inner scroll with a gold scrollbar.
  • The price (Rs. 9,900) and any other single piece of text can be edited by clicking directly on it in the editor.
  • After editing site-content.js, refresh the homepage to see your changes.
